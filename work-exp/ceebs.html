Ceebs Experience

While I was working with the disutils team, we also accepted commisions for custom bots. One of them was the Ceebs Project. Ceebs is a platform made for the Monash University which allows students to make or accepts deliveries from other students. This allows them to make a few quick bucks in their free time. I was the lead developer for a while, handling in making all the features for a few months, until I started to get burnt out while handling the commision work and disutils. That was when I had found an amazing developer from the Monash University who was interested in the project. From then on I mentored him and with his help made the development much easier.

After my team member had enough experience in working with the code base, I trusted him to take care of the project and left Ceebs. After working for about 5 months on the project, I felt my growth stagnating and couldn't give time to my personal projects, regardless, the Ceebs project was a very interesting project to work on and I'm thankful to Lachlan Nguyen who gave me opportunity. It made me learn a lot about integrating various API's and systems like Stripe, verification system and even had to make a custom API interacting with Airtable API asynchronously.
